LEADERSHIP TEAM

Affinity Sidekick was founded in March 2021 when three forward-thinking and business-oriented individuals – Tusing, Henry and Edward joined forces. The concept revolved around offering cost-efficient work and assistance for clients all over the globe. The catch and most crucial trait – everything should be done virtually, matching or possibly even exceeding the quality of work expected in-house. A couple of months later, Affinity Sidekicks can proudly claim that the idea and realization were successful. The ever-growing list of satisfied clients along with hundreds (if not thousands) saved by reducing labor costs for our partners proves the point Tusing, Henry and Edward were trying to make back in March 2021.
